Front-End Developer

Hello! My name is Michael Esfahani and I am a developer based in southern California.

I received my degree in Marine Biology from Eckerd College in St. Petersburg, Florida. I spent time working in coral aquaculture and as an aquarist before moving back to California.

One thing I enjoyed about the Marine Biology field was researching emerging science. I loved to investigate the newest science and scientific methodologies that were being developed. I see parallels in programming of what I really enjoyed in my previous experience. I found that with programming I could constantly advance my technical skills and have the potential for growth in a rapidly developing field.

My journey into programing took me from taking online coding classes, to taking a Java course at a local community college, and then eventually enrolling in an accelerated web development program which helped to cement my knowledge and teach me best practices in development.

I spend my free time training in the sport of freediving and enjoying the ocean. I enjoy traveling when I can. My top three favorite places I have traveled to are: Tonga, New Zealand, and Baja California.
